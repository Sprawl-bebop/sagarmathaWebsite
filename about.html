    About Us

    BEST Education Institution since 2003

    Sagarmatha College for Higher Studies (SMCHS), was established in 2003. It is a creation of some of Nepal’s well-known educationists, academicians, and scholars. The team is led by professionally trained and experienced teachers and staff who believe the Progressive Change in Education. It offers BCA, BBM, BA/BSW, BBS, and MBS programs of Tribhuvan University. We offer an Excellent Academic Environment with a Moderate Fee Structure. It is not only a college; it seems to be a university-based on the courses that offer. To achieve its goal, we have a multimedia classroom, well-Equipped hardware, and an 8085 model microprocessor lab For it and management students. Likewise, we have a high-speed internet lab for software and web development practices.

    Our Objectives

    1. To ensure a high standard of education.
    2. To impart value based education with emphasis on self discipline and character building.
    3. To enforce and maintain academic discipline in the campus.

    02

    Our Mission

    To strive for quality education in keeping with the motto of the college, “Excellence in Education” and prepare young minds for imbibing knowledge, skills and sensitivity.

    03

    Our Vision

    To provide affordable quality education, while equipping students with knowledge and skills in their chosen stream, inculcate values, identify hidden talents, provide opportunities for students to realize their full potential and thus shape them into future leaders, entrepreneurs and above all good human beings.
