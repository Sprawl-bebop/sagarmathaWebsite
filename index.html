    Featured Courses

    Courses We Offer

    BA/BSW

    Bachelor of Arts in Social Work is a four year academic program under Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences of Tribhuvan University.

    BBM

    Bachelor of Business Management (BBM) is a four year, eight semester program in affiliation with the Faculty of Management, Tribhuvan University. Students are required to complete 120 credit hours of course work and practicum training to graduate.

    BCA

    Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) is a four-year, 8-semester undergraduate program of Tribhuvan University (TU) under the faculties of Humanities and Social Science. This program is of 120 credit hour.
