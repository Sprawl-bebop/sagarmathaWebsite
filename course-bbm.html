    BBM Course Details

    This course is designed in such a way that this can be cornerstone for aspiring business leaders and qualified managers of future. The BBM graduates are equipped with effective knowledge, abilities, self confidence and skills to contribute in successful and efficient information management and transform themselves so as to fit in this highly changing, very competitive and volatile world.

    Eligibility Requirement

    1.He/ She should have passed +2 or any equivalent level of education from any board/ institution/ University recognized by TU.
    2. He/ she should have secured at least second division in proficiency level or intermediate level.
    3. Students should strictly fulfill all the application form requirements needed in appearing the entrance exam.

    Entrance Exam:

    Students applying for BBM must fill in the application form about their detail description of personal information and past academic records.

    The candidate selection process takes place in three phases which are as follows:
    1. Written Exam (CMAT test)
    2. Individual presentation
    3. Interview

    Duration of the Course is 4 Years
